Composed of leaders from diverse backgrounds, nationalities, culture and beliefs, our V Ambassadors are united in creating a strong network of leaders rooted in the company’s mission and vision, and devoted to changing the world through the principle of “Raise Yourself to Help Mankind” (RYTHM).

V Ambassadors facilitate global marketing events, oversee the growth and development of their respective markets. They serve as teachers and mentors to the network. They are the most accomplished professionals in Network Marketing, sharing their wisdom and travelling the world to change lives and inspire greatness in others.

An international network of marketing professionals calls for a strong core leadership that understands the challenges our people face, appreciates the value of hard work as they have experienced it themselves, and provides encouragement inspiration and motivation to the whole network.
